About Papa's Wingeria

Papa's Wingeria is a cooking game about making delicious fried chicken wings and meats at another Papa's restaurant.

How do you play Papa's Wingeria

You can play as either Chuck, Mandi, or a Custom Woker at Papa's Burgeria restaurant in Starlight City. As a worker here, you have to prepare a wide range of delicious food for the customers.

There are different kinds of foods ranging from fried foods like chicken wings, meats, to sides, and veggies.

The very first step is to receive orders at the Order Station. Next, you need to go through the other three stations including Fry, Sauce, and Build to complete an appetizing dish.

Fry up different meats, and wings, then, add sauces, and sides and serve the customers.

Try to serve even the pickiest customers and make them happy with tons of delicious wings and things.

The game gets hard when you need to deal with more than one customer at a time.

Stations:

- Order Station

- Fry Station

- Sauce Station

- Build Station

In this game, you need to prepare different types of dishes, so try to master all patterns and layouts to get high scores and tips from the customers. Your earned tips can be used to purchase upgrades in the shop.

Have fun with this fantastic sequel from the Papa Louie game series. Play it now and show off your culinary skills!

Features

- Free to play without Flash on both Desktop and Mobile

- Different dishes to prepare

- Various ingredients
