About Papa's Pastaria

Papa's Pastaria is the ninth part of the Papa Louie game series where players must cook delicious pasta for the customers that visit the restaurant.

How do you play Papa's Pastaria

The game sets in a fictional town named Portallini. You can choose to play as Doan, Utah, or a custom worker at the restaurant. Your job is to run Papa's Pastaria restaurant. You need to complete all kinds of tasks, from taking orders to preparing delicious plates of pasta. In this game, you can become a pasta chef and go through all steps to make pasta. For example, after taking orders, you need to cook noodles, add different sauces, and toppings, and create pasta exactly as the order.

Stations

- Order Station

- Cook Station

- Build Station

- Bread Station

Just like other games in this series, there are four different stations that you have to master. Try to get familiar with them as soon as possible so that you can make the food for the customers and satisfy them to earn tips and scores.

The Cook Station is also called Boiling Station. It's where you pick the right pasta type, place it in the water, and start boiling until it reaches the desired cook level of the customers.

In the Build Station, you can add sauce and toppings to the pasta.

After that, you need to move to the Bread Station to slice the bread and serve the customers.

Good luck and have fun with this fantastic cooking game!
